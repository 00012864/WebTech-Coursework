Our

Story

The restaurant's owner - Panagiotis Vassilopoulos, also the owner of ”Horizon Mills” Villas & Suites, welcomes you to the Ambrosia Restaurant and to the beautiful island.

Quality-service and unforgettable experience is the heart of the traditional Greek restaurant. The restaurant not only offers Greek cuisine enriched in international flavours, but also provides a romantic culinary experience.

The employees in Ambrosia restaurant are trained to provide professional service by adhering to the restauarnt's core values and guidelines that have developed over the past 18 years. So come and enjoy!
